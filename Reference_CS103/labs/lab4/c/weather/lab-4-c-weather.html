Weather of Hangzhou

Spring (March to May): Spring in Hangzhou is mild and pleasant, with temperatures gradually rising from around 10°C (50°F) in March to 25°C (77°F) in May. This season is known for its blooming flowers and lush greenery, making it a popular time for tourists to visit. Summer (June to August): Summers in Hangzhou are hot and humid, with temperatures often exceeding 30°C (86°F). July and August are the hottest months, with occasional thunderstorms and heavy rainfall. The humidity can make it feel even warmer, so it's essential to stay hydrated and seek shade during the peak heat. Autumn (September to November): Autumn is considered the best time to visit Hangzhou, as the weather is cool and comfortable, with temperatures ranging from 15°C (59°F) to 25°C (77°F). The city's famous West Lake is particularly picturesque during this season, with colorful foliage and clear skies. Winter (December to February): Winters in Hangzhou are cold and damp, with temperatures averaging around 5°C (41°F) but occasionally dropping below freezing. Snowfall is rare but possible. The city can be quite foggy during this time, adding a mystical charm to its landscapes.
